Prophet Prince Manasseh Atsu
Founder & Overseer · Watered Garden Church

Prophet Prince Manasseh Atsu is a prophetic minister of uncommon precision and anointing. Called from an early age, he has devoted over three decades to building the Kingdom of God through the gifts of prophecy, healing, and the uncompromising teaching of the Word.

Under his leadership, Watered Garden Church has grown from a small fellowship in Dansoman to an international ministry with branches across Ghana, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

Our History

35+ Years of Glory

Founded in 1989 in Dansoman, Accra, Watered Garden Church began as a prophetic fellowship with a mandate to manifest God's glory in every sphere of human existence. Today that mandate has carried the ministry to 6 nations and reached over 50,000 souls.
