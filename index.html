About the Mandate

The Prophet's
Heart & History

Prophet Prince Manasseh Atsu is not a man who stumbled into ministry — he was summoned by a Voice that does not make mistakes. For over three decades he has walked the narrow path of prophetic obedience, paying the price that most are unwilling to pay, to carry a glory that most cannot hold.

1989
The Founding — Dansoman, Accra
Watered Garden Church established in Dansoman with a small but fire-filled congregation. The prophetic mandate declared: reach every community, in every nation, in every generation.
2000s
Operation Church Everywhere Launched
The church planting mandate intensified — daughter churches established across the Ashanti, Western, and Greater Accra regions. The model: raise prophetic leaders, not religious dependents.
2010s
International Expansion — UK & USA
The London and Houston branches established. The Prophet's prophetic ministry recognized on the international stage — conferences, crusades, and healing meetings across two continents.
2024+
The Year of Souls — The Digital Mandate
The ministry enters its most expansive era. Jesus Festivals, 5 Days of Glory, broadcast ministry reaching 50,000+ souls. The digital cathedral rises. The mandate accelerates.
